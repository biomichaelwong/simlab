📝 DSE Exam Focus

1. Blood flows from the wider afferent arteriole to the narrower efferent arteriole.

2. This creates High Hydrostatic Pressure inside the glomerulus.

3. The high pressure forces water and small soluble molecules out of the capillaries into Bowman's capsule to form the glomerular filtrate. This process does not consume energy.
